Erling Jazz- & Ølbar

Address: Nørreport 8, Aarhus C


Erling Jazz is a charming beer bar that has been in Aarhus since 2000s celebrated for its intimate ambiance and live jazz performances.

Featuring a diverse range of craft beers, it's a must-visit destination for jazz enthusiasts and craft beer lovers exploring Aarhus.

Væskebalancen bar

Address: Jægergårdsgade 2A, Aarhus C


A cozy basement venue offering a curated selection of craft beers. With 14 rotating taps, patrons can enjoy a diverse range of brews, including both local and international options. The bar's informal and welcoming atmosphere makes it a popular spot for beer enthusiasts.

Mig & Ølsnedkeren

Address: Mejlgade 12, Aarhus C


Mig & Ølsnedkeren is a lively craft beer hub in Aarhus, renowned for its rotating taps featuring experimental brews from their Copenhagen brewery. With its rustic charm and focus on innovative beer experiences, it’s a favorite spot for locals and visitors seeking unique flavors in the city.
