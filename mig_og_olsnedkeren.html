Mig & Ølsnedkeren has been a cornerstone of Aarhus’ craft beer scene since it opened its doors in 2015. Originally an extension of the popular Ølsnedkeren brewery in Copenhagen, the Aarhus location quickly established its own identity, blending the brewery’s experimental approach with a laid-back, neighborhood vibe. Situated in the heart of Mejlgade, it became a gathering spot for beer enthusiasts looking to explore a wide variety of unique and innovative brews.

The bar is known for its ever-rotating taps, showcasing everything from hazy IPAs to barrel-aged stouts, often brewed in collaboration with other cutting-edge breweries. With its rustic interior and friendly atmosphere, Mig & Ølsnedkeren has become a must-visit for locals and visitors alike, embodying the creative spirit and community focus that define Aarhus’ craft beer culture.
