Væskebalancen Bar

Mon-Thu: 14:00-00:00

Fri-Sat: 14:00-02:00

Sun: Closed

Væskebalancen was established in early 2020 by Nick Theilgaard Olesen, co-owner of Mig & Ølsnedkeren in Mejlgade, and Lars Nielsen, an experienced beer importer. Their goal was to provide Aarhus with a dedicated bottleshop offering a diverse selection of craft beers for customers to enjoy at home. They opened their first shop in Vestergade, Aarhus, in May 2020.

The success of the Vestergade location led to the opening of a cozy basement bar at the corner of Jægergårdsgade and Frederiks Allé in spring 2022. This venue features 14 rotating taps and a selection of bottled and canned beers, creating an informal and welcoming atmosphere for beer enthusiasts.

Throughout its growth, Væskebalancen has remained committed to providing exceptional service and guidance, ensuring that customers enjoy quality beers served with a smile.
